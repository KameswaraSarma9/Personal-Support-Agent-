# Password Reset Guide

## Overview
This article explains how customers can reset their account password and what to do if the reset process fails.

## Standard Reset Steps
1. Go to the login page and click **"Forgot Password"**.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your inbox for an email titled "Reset Your Password" — it arrives within 2-3 minutes.
4. Click the reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes for security reasons.
5. Enter a new password. Passwords must be at least 10 characters and include one number and one symbol.
6. Confirm the new password and click **Save**.

## If the Reset Email Does Not Arrive
- Check your Spam or Promotions folder.
- Confirm you used the email address registered on the account (not a secondary email).
- Wait at least 5 minutes before requesting a new reset email — requesting multiple times in quick succession can delay delivery due to rate limiting.
- Add no-reply@ourapp.com to your contacts to prevent filtering.

## If the Reset Link Has Expired
Reset links are valid for 30 minutes only. If expired, return to the login page and request a new link. There is no limit to how many times you can request a new link, but each new request invalidates the previous link.

## Account Lockout After Failed Attempts
After 5 consecutive failed login attempts, the account is temporarily locked for 15 minutes as a security measure. During this lockout period, password reset requests will still work and can be used to regain access immediately.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and Password Resets
If 2FA is enabled on the account, resetting your password does not disable 2FA. You will still need your authenticator app or backup codes to complete login after the password change.

## When to Escalate
If a customer has tried all of the above steps and still cannot reset their password, or believes their account has been compromised, this should be escalated to a human agent for manual account verification.
